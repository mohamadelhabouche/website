Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Caffettino (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use our mobile apps and related services.

Effective date: January 7, 2026
Contact: support@caffettino.app

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide and information generated when you use the service, including:

- Account/profile details (e.g. name, contact details you enter)
- Order details (items, quantities, timestamps, status)
- Device and app information (app version, device identifiers)
- Notification tokens (to send order updates)
- Location (only if you enable location features)

Note: tailor this list to match your exact production behavior.

How we use information

We use information to:
- Provide and operate the service (sign-in, ordering, delivery)
- Communicate with you (order and account notifications)
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse
- Comply with legal requirements when applicable

We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

We may share information with:
- Service providers that help us run the app (e.g. hosting, analytics, messaging/notifications)
- Cafés/merchants fulfilling your order (to complete the service)
- Authorities if required by law

If you use Firebase services, mention the specific Firebase products enabled and link their policies.

Your choices

Depending on your region and how the app is configured, you may be able to:
- Access or update your profile
- Disable notifications in device settings
- Disable location permissions in device settings
- Request deletion of your account/data (contact support)

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for operational, legal, or security purposes. Retention may vary by data type (e.g. orders vs. logs).

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

The service is not intended for children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the latest version on this page and update the effective date.
