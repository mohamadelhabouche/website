What we do

Caffettino is an ordering and operations platform for modern cafés.

Better ordering

Customers browse products, place orders, and get clear status updates—without friction.

Operational clarity

Staff workflows are designed to keep orders moving smoothly and reduce mistakes.

Built for scale

From a single store to multi-branch operations, the system keeps data and roles organized.
